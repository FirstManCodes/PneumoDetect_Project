About PneumoDetect

PneumoDetect is an innovative AI-powered platform designed to assist healthcare professionals in detecting pneumonia from chest X-ray scans with precision, speed, and reliability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize pneumonia diagnosis using cutting-edge deep learning technology. We aim to reduce diagnostic errors, enhance decision-making for physicians, and ultimately save lives through early detection and intervention.

Our Vision

We envision a future where AI becomes an integral part of healthcare, ensuring affordable, accessible, and accurate diagnostics for communities around the globe, especially in regions with limited access to medical experts.

Our Story

PneumoDetect was born out of a final-year research project aimed at harnessing the power of deep learning to detect pneumonia from X-ray images. With access to large datasets and rigorous model training, we created a system that not only predicts with high accuracy but also delivers results in seconds.

This project is not just about technology; it’s about making a real-world impact in healthcare delivery by improving speed, accuracy, and accessibility of medical diagnostics.
