Our Core Features

PneumoDetect is designed to combine **cutting-edge AI technology** with a **user-friendly interface** to empower healthcare professionals and individuals alike.

AI-Powered Detection

Our deep learning model analyzes chest X-rays to detect pneumonia with high accuracy, assisting doctors in rapid and precise diagnoses.

Prediction History

Securely store and review all previous predictions in your dashboard for easy tracking and patient monitoring over time.

Accessible Anywhere

Access PneumoDetect via web on any device — whether in the hospital, clinic, or at home — without installation hassles.

Data Security

Built with security in mind, ensuring that patient data is encrypted and stored securely to comply with healthcare data regulations.

PDF Reports

Generate downloadable PDF reports of predictions for documentation and sharing with patients or other medical professionals.

Real-Time Accuracy Metrics

View real-time model accuracy and performance metrics, including confusion matrix, directly in the app.
